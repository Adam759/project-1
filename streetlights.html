Street Lights, What's The Big Deal?

You may be wondering, this is an odd page to be in a web design project? The specifications said the other two pages could be whatever we liked, so I chose this! I do a lot of work on industrial lighting and have worked on roadway illumination. For some reason lighting of all sorts is interesting to me and is a lot more involved than most people think. I collect and work on a lot of lighting fixtues, primarily street and roadway lights. I thought I would take this opportunity to share some of the common types of roadway lights. Not a lot of people are aware but roadway illumination techology is changing fast and much of the older technology is fading into history, of which I want to make sure is not forgotten.
